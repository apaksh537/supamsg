## Reminders

- Respond to every PH comment — engagement boosts ranking
- Be genuine, not salesy — PH community values authenticity
- If something breaks, fix it FAST and post an update comment
- Don't ask for upvotes directly — ask people to "check it out and share feedback"
- Share real numbers (downloads, signups) in comments — PH loves transparency
