Manage all your WhatsApp accounts from one beautiful window. No more switching between phones.

Getting Started

Setting up is simple — just scan QR codes like you would for WhatsApp Web.

  • 1 Each account shows a WhatsApp Web QR code
  • 2 Open WhatsApp on the phone for that number
  • 3 Go to Linked Devices and scan the QR code
  • 4 Repeat for each phone — you're done!

Keyboard Shortcuts

Power through your messages with these shortcuts.

Cmd+1-9 Switch accounts
Cmd+\ Toggle sidebar
Cmd+D Split screen
Ctrl+Shift+W Show/hide app
Cmd+T Templates
Cmd+E Export chat
